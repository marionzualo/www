Hei!

I'm a software developer and I spend most of my time building systems for the web. I work at Seedrs.

I'm particularly curious about curation. Due to the abundance of content on the web, I often wonder about ways to discover awesome things without much effort.

I love street art and how it transforms cities into open canvases. I am concerned about its preservation and frequently ask myself how much of it will be around 100 years from now.

I actively practice dance - Hip Hop, House and Locking are my favourite styles. My other interests include tinkering with side projects, reading and writing.
